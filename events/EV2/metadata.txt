Title: Cybersecurity Awareness Workshop

Date: 18 July 2026

Department Responsible:
Information Technology Department

The Information Technology Department conducted a one-day cybersecurity awareness workshop for graduate engineer trainees and summer interns.

The workshop focused on password security, phishing attacks, ransomware, email fraud, secure coding practices, VPN usage, and organizational data protection.

Mr. Rajiv Menon, Senior Network Administrator, demonstrated multiple real-world cyberattack scenarios using a controlled lab environment.

Hands-on exercises included:
- Identifying phishing emails
- Creating strong passwords
- Multi-factor authentication setup
- Secure browsing practices
