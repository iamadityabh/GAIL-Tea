Artificial Intelligence Department

Project Showcase and Innovation Day

On 12 September 2026, the Artificial Intelligence Department organized an internal Project Showcase at the GAIL Training Institute Innovation Lab.

The event allowed trainees to demonstrate AI-based solutions developed during their internship.

Projects presented included:

• Predictive Equipment Maintenance using Machine Learning
• AI Chatbot for Training Management
• Document Search using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
• Attendance Analytics Dashboard
• Image-based Safety Helmet Detection

Each team was given 15 minutes for presentation followed by a 10-minute evaluation session.

The judging panel consisted of senior scientists, department mentors, and invited industry experts.

Awards:
Best Innovation
Best Technical Implementation
Best Presentation

Coordinator:
Dr. Amit Verma

Venue:
Innovation Lab

Number of Presenting Teams:
18

Audience:
Faculty members, interns, department heads, invited guests.

The event ended with feedback from judges and a discussion on future AI applications within GAIL.
