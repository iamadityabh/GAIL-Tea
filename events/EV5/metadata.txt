Annual Inter-Department Training Conclave 2026

The GAIL Training Institute organized its Annual Inter-Department Training Conclave on 15 November 2026.

Unlike regular workshops, this event involved all four departments:
Human Resources Management
Information Technology
Artificial Intelligence
Marketing

The objective was to review training activities conducted throughout the year, recognize outstanding trainee achievements, and encourage collaboration across departments.

Morning Session

Institute Director's Address

Department-wise Annual Report

Recognition of Best Mentors

Presentation of Outstanding Trainee Projects

Afternoon Session

Panel Discussion:
"The Future of AI and Digital Transformation in the Energy Sector"

Networking Lunch

Poster Exhibition

Feedback Collection

Closing Ceremony

Special Guests

Executive Director (Human Resources)

Chief General Manager (IT)

Head of Artificial Intelligence Division

Senior General Manager (Marketing)

Venue:
Central Convention Hall

Attendance:
Approximately 250 participants

Key Outcomes

Cross-department collaboration opportunities were identified.

Multiple trainee projects were recommended for further development.

A proposal was announced to organize quarterly innovation workshops starting next year.
