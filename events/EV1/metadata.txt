Event Name: Summer Internship Orientation 2026

The Human Resources Management Department organized the Summer Internship Orientation Program on 10 June 2026 at the GAIL Training Institute Auditorium.

The objective of the session was to welcome newly joined interns and familiarize them with the institute's policies, code of conduct, training schedule, project allocation process, attendance rules, and safety guidelines.

The program started at 9:30 AM with a welcome address by the HR Head. This was followed by an introduction of mentors from all departments including IT, AI, Marketing, and Human Resources Management.

After the introduction, interns were briefed about the facilities available in the institute, reporting procedures, leave policies, and documentation requirements.

A networking session was held after lunch where interns interacted with mentors and previous batch trainees.

The event concluded at 4:00 PM.

Venue:
Main Auditorium, GAIL Training Institute

Coordinator:
Ms. Neha Sharma

Expected Participants:
120 interns

Important Notes:
- Formal dress code was mandatory.
- Identity cards were distributed during registration.
- Attendance was compulsory.

Departments Involved:
Human Resources Management, IT, AI, Marketing
