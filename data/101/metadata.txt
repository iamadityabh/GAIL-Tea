The trainee is currently undergoing industrial training at the GAIL Training Institute, Noida, and is actively working on the development of an AI-powered chatbot system intended to automate routine departmental tasks and improve information accessibility for employees. The project focuses on reducing the time spent handling repetitive queries related to HR policies, training schedules, leave management, document retrieval, and general administrative procedures. The trainee is presently engaged in the third phase of the project, Development and Integration, under the supervision of mentor Ms. Neha Sharma, Assistant Manager (IT).

The project follows a structured five-phase development lifecycle consisting of Requirement Analysis, System Design, Development and Integration, Testing and Bug Resolution, and Deployment with Performance Monitoring. During the requirement analysis phase, data was collected from various departments to identify common employee queries and workflow bottlenecks. The design phase involved creating conversation flows, defining chatbot architecture, and planning database connectivity. The current development phase focuses on implementing NLP-based query processing, backend integration, and response management modules.

The chatbot is being designed to support real-time query resolution, user authentication, document search functionality, and personalized responses based on user roles. The trainee has contributed to creating intent-response datasets, testing conversational workflows, integrating backend databases, and preparing technical documentation. Weekly review sessions are conducted with the project mentor to evaluate progress and discuss potential improvements in chatbot accuracy and usability.

The project team comprises five trainees working collaboratively on different modules, including frontend development, backend services, AI model training, testing, and deployment planning. Additional responsibilities assigned to the trainee include maintaining development logs, documenting test cases, analyzing user feedback, and monitoring chatbot performance metrics. Upon completion, the system is expected to be deployed on the organization's internal network and integrated with existing information management systems. Through this project, the trainee is gaining valuable hands-on experience in artificial intelligence applications, natural language processing, software development practices, system integration, and project management within a professional industrial environment.
